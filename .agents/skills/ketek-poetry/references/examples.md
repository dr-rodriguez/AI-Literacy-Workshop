# Ketek Poetry Examples

These examples demonstrate the structure and tone of a Ketek. Each section is separated by a forward slash (`/`).

## Classic Ketek (The Way of Kings)
"Above silence, the illuminating storms—dying storms—illuminate the silence above."
**Formatted:** Above silence / the illuminating storms / dying storms / illuminate / the silence above.

## Allahn's Ketek
"Radiant / of birthplace / the announcer comes / to come announce / the birthplace of Radiants."
**Formatted:** Radiant / of birthplace / the announcer comes / to come announce / the birthplace of Radiants.

## Navani's Ketek (Words of Radiance)
"Alight, winds approach deadly approaching winds alight."
**Formatted:** Alight / winds approach / deadly / approaching winds / alight.

## Navani's Ketek (Mourning)
"You, always about dreams. My soul weeps. Farewell, weeping soul. My dreams . . . about, always, You."
**Formatted:** You, always about dreams / My soul weeps / Farewell / weeping soul / My dreams about, always, You.

## Jasnah's Ketek
"United, new beginnings sing: 'Defying truth, love. Truth defy!' Sing beginnings, new unity."
**Formatted:** United, new beginnings sing / Defying truth, love / Truth defy! / Sing beginnings / new unity.

## El's Ketek (Rhythm of War)
"Burdens, Our Calling. Songs of Home, a knowledge: Knowing a Home of Songs, called our burden."
**Formatted:** Burdens / Our Calling / Songs of Home / a knowledge / Knowing a Home of Songs / called our burden.

## Urithiru Inscription (Dalinar's Ketek?)
"Trusted Words, Known Words, Renounce Once. Renounce Words Known. Words…Trusted… Once."
**Formatted:** Trusted Words, Known Words / Renounce Once / Renounce / Words Known / Words…Trusted… Once.

## Fan Example: Life and Death
"Life brings death, a cycle ending; end the cycle, death brings life."
**Formatted:** Life brings death / a cycle ending / end the cycle / death brings / life.

## Fan Example: Light and Shadow
"Light creates shadow, hidden truth; truly hiding shadows, creating light."
**Formatted:** Light creates shadow / hidden truth / truly hiding / shadows / creating light.
